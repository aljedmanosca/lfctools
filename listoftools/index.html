    List of Tools

    Below are the list of LFCtools. Most are found in the Excel ribbon, some are accessible through the right click menu and some are hidden and can be activated through combined keypress.

    Zoom

    Allows to quickly change between excel zoom levels: 100% - 85% - 75% - 60%

    Highlighter

    Allows to quickly assign color fill to selected cells or range
